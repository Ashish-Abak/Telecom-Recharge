About Us





Our Mission

Reliance Jio promises to shape the future of India by providing end-to-end digital solutions for businesses,
institutions and households and seamlessly bridging the rural-urban divide.





Our Vision

Reliances vision for India is that broadband and digital services will no longer be a luxury item.
Rather, Reliance envisions an India where these are basic necessities to be consumed in abundance
by consumers and small businesses alike, as much in far-flung villages as in our largest cities.
The initiatives are truly aligned with the Government of India's Digital India vision for our nation.
